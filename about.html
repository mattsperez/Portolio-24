About Me

After spending 15 years as a Chef, I decided to take a break and enroll in a Full Stack Bootcamp to learn coding. During this process, I developed a strong interest in Frontend development. Graphic design has always been my passion, and Frontend development became a stepping stone to a new career path. My previous experience as a Chef has been helpful in learning new programming languages. It's similar to starting in a kitchen that prepares cuisine you're not familiar with. Once you've learned the concept, everything starts to make sense. Even if I'm not always comfortable with it, I'll keep trying to improve.
